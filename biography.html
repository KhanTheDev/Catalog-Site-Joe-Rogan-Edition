Biography

Early Life & Career

Joe Rogan is an American comedian, podcast host, and MMA commentator whose journey began in stand‐up comedy. Over the years, he has transformed his unique point of view into one of the most listened‐to podcasts in the world.

UFC & MMA Commentary

Since joining the UFC in the late 1990s, Joe Rogan’s in‐depth commentary and passion for martial arts have reshaped the way fans experience MMA. His enthusiastic style and technical insight make him a favorite among viewers worldwide.
